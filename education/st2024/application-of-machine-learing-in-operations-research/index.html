Application of Machine Learning in Operations Research, Summer Term 2024

Material

Overview

Solving problems in Operations Research (OR) often requires significant amounts of domain knowledge. Recently, Machine Learning (ML) was adopted in OR to address this challenge. In this seminar, we want to investigate approaches that combine both fields, ML and OR, for solving optimization problems.

This block seminar course will be held in English.

Prerequisites

Basic knowledge about Operations Research e.g. Mixed-Integer-Linear-Programming. Basic knowledge about Machine Learning.
