ADA AutoAI Workshop, 9 December 2022

General information

The ADA workshop on AutoAI will take place in-person in the seminar room (6)228 of Informatik 6 at Theaterstraße 35-39, 52062 Aachen, 2nd floor. Please arrive on time since we have a tight workshop schedule!

There are multiple sessions with flash-talks (up to ten minutes) with subsequent short discussions. Holger Hoos is the session chair who is responsible to keep track of the time and leads the discussion.
